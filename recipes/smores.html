Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Chocolate bar
  • Marshmallows
  1. Snap a graham cracker in half the width/short way.
  2. Put a similar size piece of chocolate on one graham cracker half
  3. Roast Marshmallow over open flame
  4. once toasted golden brown, make sandwich chocolate and marshmallow with the graham cracker as ends of the 'bread' of the sandwich
